# Mini-Max Sum

## Problem

Given five positive integers, find the minimum and maximum values that can be calculated by summing exactly four of the five integers.

Print the minimum sum and the maximum sum.

---

## Example

Input:

arr = [1,2,3,4,5]

Output:

10 14

Explanation:

1 + 2 + 3 + 4 = 10

2 + 3 + 4 + 5 = 14

---

## Constraints

- arr.length = 5
- 1 <= arr[i] <= 10^9
